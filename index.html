INTRODUCTION

Hello, I'm Onuoha. I've been designing things for years but only as recently as a year ago, I decided to learn coding. I can also design and prototype UI. The projects in this portfolio are all inspired by my imagination.

I take great pride in not only being able to complete work to the high standard you expect but also go the extra mile wherever possible. With a business administration degree and more than Five years of experience in operations, I offer a unique combination of knowledge, expertise and technical skills to help your organisation achieve its goals.

Lately, I've discovered my passion for the world of web development and the beauty behind building useful websites like the ones you will find in this portfolio. I'm now looking for a FrontEnd Developer position to finally kick start my career and learn among professionals.
